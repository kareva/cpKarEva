Eva's Top 5 Winter Essentials

November 27, 2017



Hello there and welcome back to KarEva Beauty. Today I'll be sharing my top 5 winter essentials with you. This will include some of the hacks I have learned from trying to adjust to Canadian winters. How you can be warm and look stylish at the same time.





For starters the most important essential for surviving cold winters is a pair of good shoes. My personal favourite are the Pajar winter boots. When your feet are warm, you are too! It is also important to make sure they have a good grip, nobody likes slipping on the ice infront of people. Another common pair that people seem to wear would be the Sorel boots.





Having a warm coat is also very important. This entails having a coat that is thick and long, it is also recommended to have a hood, for the snow storms. It is key for it to be long, without that the wind will blow up it and make you cold. My personal favourites are london fog coats. The one I own is in red to mix it up a bit.





Accesorizing with hats and scarves makes you stay extra warm but also adds a fashionable sense to your coat and boots.





Thick socks on a cold day is a must!





Finally, we all have those moments when our eyes water in the cold winter weather. My advise would be to wear a mascara that is good quality, my personal favourite is the Marc Jacobs black mascara. For me waterproof mascara is too hard to remove.
