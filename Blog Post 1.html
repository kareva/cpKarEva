Karina's Top 5 Makeup Products

November 20, 2017



Hi ! Welcome back to KarEva Beauty. Today I thought that I would share with you guys my top 5 favourite makeup products. These include my everyday makeup essentials, which are the perfect product to help you create an everyday natural look.

To purchase any of the products that I have included, simply click on the image of the product and you will be directed to the website to purchase the exact product that I use! Hope you enjoy.





My first favourite makeup product, is the Tarte Tartlette In Bloom Palette. This eyeshadow palette comes with 12 eyeshadows, with various ranges of natural shades and includes both shimmer and matte shadows. I use the palette on the regular, to create my everyday natural eye looks or even a dark smoky eye. This palette is great and you can create many different eye shadow looks with this one palette.



Price: $60.00 - Purchase at Sephora

The picture below, displays swatches for half the shades in the palette, but these 6 are my favourite. I often use funny girl or firecracker on the lid. Then I apply jetsetter in the crease and finally use rebel, leader, and activist to darken the outer crease. Follow these steps and you can create the perfect eye look!





From left to right the names of the shades are: Jetsetter, Funny Girl, Rebel, Firecracker, Leader, and Activist







The foundation I use every single day is the Too Faced Born This Way Foundation. I cannot stop using it!! It provides a medium to full coverage, to create the perfect base for your makeup – providing you with a flawless complexion. The packaging is so cute, you cannot go wrong with this product. The foundation also comes in 24 different shades ranging from light to dark so I’m sure you will be able to find the right shade for your complexion!

Price: $49.00 - Purchase at Sephora





To get that bronzed look, I use Benefit Cosmetics Hoola Bronzer! It gives the perfect bronzed glow to the skin. This is great to use now as the winter months are approaching and everyone can use a nice bronze to their complexion. I use the original Hoola Bronzer shade and I have a medium complexion. If you are fair, the bronzer does come in a lighter shade called Hoola Lite, which is perfect for those with lighter skin tones.

Price: $38.00 - Purchase at Sephora





One can never forget mascara! It completes any look. My all-time favourite is the Maybelline Lash Sensation Waterproof Mascara. This mascara is perfect, it lengthens and volumizes your eyelashes to give a false like effect. This is one of the best waterproof mascara’s I have used, even my makeup remover couldn’t take it off so I’m sure it will last you all day. Be sure to pick up a waterproof makeup remover – it is a lifesaver because this mascara is hard to get off! I would definitely recommend that you purchase this product as it is a staple product in my routine.

Price: $5.96 - Purchase at WalMart or Shoppers Drug Mart





Lastly, for my lips I use the MAC Lip Liner in the shade Whirl. This is a classic in the beauty world, ever since Kylie Jenner announced that she uses this product to get her perfect natural lip colour. It looks amazing on Kylie and this shade works really well for practically any skin type, it's a great universal shade that can suit many skintones. It's the perfect lip colour and will look great on everyone!

Price: $22.00 - Purchase at MAC Cosmetics



Since Christmas is coming up soon, these products would be perfect gifts to give to a friend or family member that is a makeup addict. The products are very good and would make the perfect gift or if you want to treat yourself to some new makeup over the holidays, these are some great products!

Below, I have attached a link to my favourite beauty YouTuber, Desi Perkins, who is an amazing beauty influencer and is great at teaching how to apply makeup. I recommend purchasing the products I have recommended above and follow the steps she takes in this video to achieve the perfect everyday natural makeup look!



Hope you all enjoy the YouTube video of Desi Perkins! I can't wait to come back with another blog post. Talk to you soon !!

- Karina Chin
