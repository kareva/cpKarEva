About

We're Karina and Evangelia and we are the creators of KarEva Beauty. We are two passionate girls interested in beauty and fashion. About - Karina Chin

Hi! My name is Karina Chin and I am the co-founder of KarEva Beauty. I am so excited to begin creating and sharing these blog posts with you all. Here's a little more information about myself. I'm from Markham, ON but I am currently living in Waterloo, ON. Where I am attending Wilfrid Laurier University studying Business Administration. Growing up I have always enjoyed all things beauty and fashion related, as I think it is so much fun to test new makeup and shop the latest fashion trends. When I'm not at school or working on this blog, I enjoy dancing and baking. I have been a competitive dancer for over 8+ years and baking is another passion of mine since I was really young. Who doesn't love cookies, cupcakes, tarts and more! I can't wait to begin this journey with you all!!

- Karina Chin

About - Evangelia Kousthana

Hello, my name is Evangelia, I am the co-founder of the KarEva Beauty Blog. I was born and raised on the Greek island of Mykonos where a lot of Hollywood stars enjoy coming for vacation which led me to take a particular interest in beauty and fashion. I moved to Canada a year and a half ago to attend Wilfrid Laurier University for a General Science Degree and Conestoga College for a diploma in American Sign Language. In my spare time I enjoy sketching, sewing, going to the beach and of course shopping. I hope you will enjoy this blog as much as I do!
